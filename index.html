Biography

I am a undergraduate from Flager College with a degree in International Studies and a focus in Political Science.

I first became interested in coding 6 years ago when I realized there wasn't any single-program or application to find local bands and live music at establishments nearby, which is now the very popular "Events" module on Facebook. Realizing I needed much more experience than drag-and-dropping through wordpress, I took time off to finish my school studies.

Fast-forward to now, after diligent and persistent self-studies through FreeCodeCamp, Codecademy, and Udemy - I've developed a few small apps and taken a company and expanded their non-existent online presence to occupy space across multiple social media platforms alongside designing and deploying a fully responsive mobile-first website. With my help, they have increased their customer engagement by over 150%.

My interests lie in creating beautiful front-end ecosystems with modern, dynamic user experiences.
