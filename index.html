Biography

I have two years experience in Front End Web Development alongside design expertise working as the sole designer and developer for an Alarm Systems company.

I first became interested in coding 6 years ago when I realized there wasn't any single-program or application to find local bands and live music at establishments nearby, which is now the very popular "Events" module on Facebook.

Fast-forward, I've developed a few personal apps and taken a company and expanded their non-existent online presence to occupy space across multiple social media platforms while designing, developing, and deploying a fully responsive mobile-first website with JavaScript, jQuery, HTML/CSS. With a prospering online presence, they have increased their digital customer engagement by over 300%.

My passion lies in brainstorming and creating sleek front-end interfaces with modern, dynamic user experiences using JavaScript and React. I also have a passionate, keen eye for design which I like to experiment with in my personal projects.

Since I've said Hello to the World, I've been hooked ever since, striving to grow and learn to become the best developer I can be. The skills in my resume and the features & projects listed here will continue to evolve and expand, so check back soon to see changes.

My hobbies outside of coding include surfing, rock climbing, guitar, hiking, yoga, and reading.
